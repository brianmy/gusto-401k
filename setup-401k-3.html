Step 3 of 3 — Confirm enrollment

Review your enrollment settings

These settings meet SECURE 2.0 requirements and are recommended for New York mandate compliance. All can be adjusted after activation.

Auto-enrollment: 3% of compensation
Required by SECURE 2.0 for plans created after Dec 29, 2022. Each employee is enrolled automatically at 3% of pay. They can adjust their contribution or opt out anytime from their Gusto account.
Annual contribution escalation: 1% per year, up to 10%
Required by SECURE 2.0. Employee contributions increase by 1% each year until reaching 10%. Employees can opt out of escalation separately from auto-enrollment.
Safe harbor plan: non-discrimination testing waived
Eliminates ADP/ACP non-discrimination testing, so you can max your own contributions — up to $70,000 — regardless of how much your employees contribute. Requires minimum employer contribution or match.
Employee opt-out: always available
Any employee can adjust their contribution rate or opt out entirely from their own Gusto account at any time. No action required from you as the employer.
